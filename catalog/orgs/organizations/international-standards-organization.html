ISO

  • ISO 00-09 "Core Standards"

    • ISO 00 "World Management"

      • ISO 00.00 "Chunk Pruning"
        "Chunk Pruning" is the process of removing "Inactive Chunks"
        • ISO-00.00.00 "Chunk Pruning Process"
        • ISO-00.00.01 "Inactive Chunks"
          "Inactive Chunks" are chunks outside of the "Dimension"'s boundaries
        • ISO-00.00.02 "Why Prune Chunks?"
          Chunks are pruned for two reasons:
          1. To keep the world size managable
          2. To make non-renewable resources renewable

    • ISO 01 "Dimensions"

      • ISO 01.00 "Overworld"
        • ISO 01.00.00 "Overworld Boundaries"
          The Overworld is ~10000x~10000 blocks, that means anything further out than ~5000 blocks will be "Pruned"
      • ISO 01.01 "Nether"
        • ISO 01.01.00 "Nether Boundaries"
          The Nether is ~3000x~3000 blocks, that means anything further out than ~1500 blocks will be "Pruned"
      • ISO 01.02 "End"
        • ISO 01.02.00 "End Boundaries"
          The End is ~20000x~20000 blocks, that means anything further out than ~10000 blocks will be "Pruned"

    • ISO 02 "Organizations"

      An "Organization" is an identity which its members can perform actions "In the name of".
